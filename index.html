Welcome to our Second edition of
the Golden Age TangO (GATO) Marathon!

Due to popular demand, we are celebrating "Return of the GATO" in October again this year.

Come join us as we celebrate our life journeys over a fun and intimate tango marathon in San Diego with an eclectic mix of dancers from across North America and beyond!

Join us for a weekend of great dancing to exquisite music from some of the best DJs:

  • Ayano Yoneda (San Francisco, CA)
  • Derek Tang (Los Angeles, CA)
  • Georges Naasan (New York, NY)
  • Kim Dai (New York, NY)
  • Ramu Pyreddy (Washington, DC)
  • Sabah Chammas (San Diego, CA)

Full marathon pass (6 milongas) $175 per person (early bird price)
